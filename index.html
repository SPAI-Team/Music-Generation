About SPAI!

Who are we?

We are a group of students with like minded interests in AI. As a club we organise AI related workshops to teach others more about AI as well as raise awareness for the usage of artificial intelligence. We have worked on several projects. For instance , Machine Learning beginner bootcamp , AI Talks and Machine Learning Advanced Bootcamp for SPAI members to learn more about Artificial Intelligence other than in the classroom.

What We Do

We provide chances for new members to learn about Artificial Intelligence with different events such as Machine Learning Bootcamp and AI Talks.

Life in SPAI

Members of SPAI can participate in various activities organised by SPAI and are informed of any up coming events. The Subcommittee of SPAI organises the events and comes up with interesting events for members of SPAI.
